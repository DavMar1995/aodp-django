The correlation between CO2 and GDP

The purpose of this report is twofold. First, by data visualization in the economic, environment, and life quality, we hope to provide a country profile of AODP (Asia Open Data Partnership) members. By so doing, AODP can initiate issues for data development strategies that better fit to members' needs and situations. Second, all AODP members except Taiwan are members of the United Nation and their data of World Development Indicators (WDI) for Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) can be easily accessed from portals such as World Bank Open Data (https://data.worldbank.org). As a core member and the initiator of AODP, this report can help people have a deeper understanding of Taiwan by comparing the WDI of Taiwan with that of other Asian countries.
